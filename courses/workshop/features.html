Forever loop


What if you want your robot to drive forwards and turn right forever?
Well… Until the battery dies.

In that case you would use a forever loop(also known as a while loop). This makes your program run repeat itself forever. A forever loop makes life a lot easier in terms of not having to write lots and lots of lines of code for repeated functions.
